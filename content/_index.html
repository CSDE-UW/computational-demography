2020-04-30: Chuck Lanfear. Safe Graph Mobility Data.

The computational demography working group will meet virtually this quarter! Please join us on Thursday, 4/30, at 12pm for a presentation and discussion of SafeGraph Mobility Data. Sociology graduate student Chuck Lanfear will demonstrate a digital trace data set for mobility research obtained by Adrian Dobra of UW Statistics and CS&SS. They are soliciting new project and collaborations ideas for these unique data.
