Meet a Social Scientist - Matt Rafalow, Google

   2021-02-01      Zack Almquist      Professional Development     

Bio Matt Rafalow is a Visiting Scholar at UC Berkeley’s Center for Science, Technology, Medicine and Society, and a social scientist at Google. His book, Digital Divisions: How Schools Create Inequality in the Tech Era, just published with University of Chicago Press. He is also co-author of Affinity Online: How Connection and Shared Interests Fuel Learning (NYU Press, 2018). His work has appeared in journals such as American Journal of Sociology, Symbolic Interaction, and Social Currents.

Meet a Data Scientist - Haema Nilakanta

   2021-01-25      Zack Almquist      Professional Development      target

Bio Haema Nilakanta received her PhD from the University of Minnesota, School of Statistics. Her research interests include Monte Carlo theory and methods, computational statistics, network analysis, and statistical applications to public health and population studies. Haema is based in Minneapolis and is currently an AI Scientist for Target.
