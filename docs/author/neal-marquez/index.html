Migration Networks

   2020-02-27      Neal Marquez      presentation      Migration · Bayesian Modeling

Emanuela Furfaro presented her work on accounting for immigration policies in the analysis of migration networks. Here’s the abstract for Emanuela’s presentation: In this work, we apply statistical network models to study the effects of macro factors on determining international migration flows from developing countries to a group of selected Western European countries. Besides the traditional pull and push effects, gravity-related variables and cultural similarities, we investigate the role of immigration policies in explaining the network structure.
