About the CDWG

The Computational Demography Working Group (CDWG) at the University of Washington meets several times a quarter to provide an interdisciplinary forum for discussions of digital and computational approaches to demographic research. The workshop features a range of paper presentations, methods demonstrations and software tutorials. The CDWG is sponsored by the Center for Studies in Demography and Ecology and the eScience Institute. We welcome anyone with interest in computational demography, broadly defined!
